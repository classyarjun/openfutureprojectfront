Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Mr. Nikhileshwar Kshirsagar

Nikhileshwar Kshirsagar, the CEO of OpenFuture Pvt. Ltd., is a visionary leader renowned for his dedication to youth empowerment and technological innovation. Driven by a mission to create opportunities and tackle unemployment, he has founded multiple initiatives that focus on skill development, employment generation, and fostering a culture of excellence. In 2016, he founded Dreamland For Youth, a platform dedicated to equipping young individuals with the skills and resources needed to thrive in competitive job markets. Building on this vision, he later established Nikhileshwar Engineering Academy in Amravati, providing aspiring professionals with practical training and real-world job opportunities. In 2021, Nikhileshwar launched QuantumSoft Technologies Pvt. Ltd., a company celebrated for its employee-centric policies, including a 0% layoff rate, and its focus on delivering innovative IT solutions. Under his leadership, OpenFuture Group has achieved remarkable milestones, becoming a leading name in the IT sector. His remarkable achievements have been widely recognized. In 2023, Nikhileshwar was honored with the prestigious Fortune's Businessperson of the Year award, a testament to his exceptional leadership, business acumen, and impact on the industry. Acknowledged by the Government of Maharashtra and other esteemed organizations for his contributions, Nikhileshwar Kshirsagar continues to inspire others with his transformative leadership, commitment to social impact, and relentless pursuit of excellence.
